About Jennifer

Jennifer is a name synonymous with sophistication and luxury. Founded by Daniel Soufi, Jennifer crafts timeless, premium garments made from the finest materials including organic silk, pure cotton, and the best European fabrics. Our collections aim to offer an elegant solution for women who demand only the best in fashion, blending classic designs with modern sensibilities. Jennifer is available through exclusive stores worldwide and our online platform.
