About Jennifer

Founded by Daniel Soufi, Jennifer is a global brand rooted in the rich traditions of European luxury. Using only the finest materials including organic silk, pure cotton, and top-tier European fabrics, Jennifer competes with the world’s most prestigious fashion houses. Our products are designed to offer elegance, sophistication, and timeless style.
